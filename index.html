Stockholm

About

Stockholm Municipality or the City of Stockholm (Stockholms stad) is a municipality in east central Sweden. It has the largest population of the 290 municipalities of the country, but one of the smallest areas, making it the most densely populated.

Geographically, the municipality comprises the Stockholm City Centre and two suburban areas, Söderort (South Stockholm) and Västerort (West Stockholm). Administratively, it is subdivided into 14 districts, which are administered by district councils (stadsdelsnämnder).
